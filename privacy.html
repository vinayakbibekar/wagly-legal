Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Wagly ("we", "us", "the app") is operated by Vinayak Bibekar (sole proprietor, based in India). This page explains what we collect, why, who we share it with, and your rights.

By using Wagly you agree to this policy. If you don't agree, please don't use the app.

Data we collect

When you sign up and use Wagly, we store the following on Supabase Inc. servers in the Asia-Pacific (Mumbai) region:

  • Account — email, hashed password (never plaintext), display name (if you provide one), Supabase user id. If you sign in with Google, Google additionally shares your name and profile picture URL with us.
  • Dog profiles — name, breed, birthday, gotcha day, weight, sex, microchip id, photo (if you upload one), notes.
  • Walks — GPS route polyline, start/end timestamps, distance, duration, weather notes, poop/pee count, optional photos. Location is recorded only while you actively press "Start walk." It is not collected in the background.
  • Care & health logs (only what you choose to log) — reminders, vaccinations, medications, vet appointments, weight entries, feeding schedules, bathroom log, training progress, photos of vaccination cards, prescriptions and vet reports.
  • Device & app data — app version, OS version, device model, Expo push token, anonymous session id.
  • Usage events — which screens you open, when you start/finish a walk, when you complete onboarding, which features you tap "Notify me" on. No personal content is stored in event data.
  • Crash reports — error message, stack trace, recent app actions. Sent only when the app crashes.

We do not collect:

  • Contacts, calendar, microphone, accessibility data, SMS, call logs.
  • Location data outside of an active walk recording.
  • Data from other apps on your device.
  • Biometric data, financial data, or precise health diagnostics.

How we use the data

  • To provide the app's features (walk tracking, reminders, health timeline).
  • To send push notifications you opt into.
  • To diagnose crashes and fix bugs.
  • To understand which features people use, in aggregate.

We do not sell your data, and we do not show advertising in the app.

Sharing

Your data is processed by these third parties strictly to operate Wagly:

  • Supabase Inc. (USA) — database, authentication, storage, edge functions. Privacy policy
  • Expo / 650 Industries Inc. (USA) — over-the-air updates and push notification delivery. Privacy policy
  • Google LLC (USA) — only if you choose "Continue with Google" for sign-in. We receive your email, name and profile picture. Privacy policy

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade data with anyone else. We will disclose data if compelled by a valid legal order from an Indian court or competent authority.

Data security

  • All traffic between the app and Supabase is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+).
  • Passwords are stored hashed with bcrypt (Supabase Auth default).
  • Photos and reports are stored in Supabase Storage with row-level security scoped to your user id.
  • We do not store API keys or service-role secrets in the mobile app.

No system is perfectly secure. If we ever suffer a data incident affecting your personal data, we will notify you within 72 hours of becoming aware, in accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India).

Your rights

Under the DPDP Act 2023 (India), GDPR (EU/UK), and similar laws, you can:

  • Access — see all the data we hold on you. Email us; we'll send a JSON export within 30 days.
  • Correction — edit any wrong information directly in the app, or email us.
  • Erasure — delete your account from Profile → Settings → Delete account. All your dogs, walks, reminders, photos, and account record are erased within 30 days.
  • Withdraw consent — disable push notifications in Profile → Settings or your OS settings.
  • Data portability — receive your data in machine-readable JSON.
  • Lodge a complaint — contact India's Data Protection Board (once appointed) or, if you live in the EU/UK, your local supervisory authority.

Data retention

Data is retained as long as your account is active. After account deletion:

  • All personal records are purged within 30 days.
  • Crash reports tied to your account are anonymized.
  • Backup retention: up to 7 additional days.

Children

Wagly is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has used Wagly, contact us and we will delete the account immediately.

Changes to this policy

If we change anything material, we'll show an in-app notice on next launch and email registered users. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the revision.
